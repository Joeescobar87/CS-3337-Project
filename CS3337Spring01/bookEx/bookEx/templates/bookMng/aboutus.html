    About Us

    As a collective group of computer science students and book enthusiasts , we sought to create a place where others could exchange books for cash. Regardless of what book you want to sell, we wanted to create a massive library to for those to find cheap and affordable books.

    With those ideals in mind, for any transactions we wanted to create a user-controlled market where the user-sellers hold the control to price their books this is done to minimize any costs that goes towards purchasing through our website. Here we sought to not only build a community but to help those in pursuit of finding the reading material they are looking for.
